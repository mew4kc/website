About X - Culture:

X-Culture is an international business collaboration and consulting project. Thousands of students from around the world work in teams to solve challenges from real international businesses.
ZENCV is an online platform for professional resume design & writing services that will match customers with freelancers like top-notch CV designers and HR/industry experts to create high-quality, custom-made CVs.
The goal of this project was to aid ZenCV in their market expansion strategy. In our approach we completed market research, competition analysis, and product development, among other tasks.
Our team was selected to be the recipients of the 2020-1 X-Culture Best Team Award.
